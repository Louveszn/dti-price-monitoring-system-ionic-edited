About

CPD Price Tracker is an intuitive analytical tool designed to simplify market monitoring by automatically comparing current and historical product pricing from your uploaded files. Our system features advanced comparison logic that identifies price fluctuations and generates a comprehensive summary dashboard, highlighting the total number of increases and decreases along with the products experiencing the most significant price shifts. To streamline your workflow, the platform allows you to instantly export these insights into a professional Word report for easy sharing and record-keeping.
